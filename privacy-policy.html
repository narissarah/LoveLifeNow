Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Love Life Now Foundation ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, and supporters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website at lovelifenow.org and our online forms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Donation information: donation amounts and payment details (processed securely through our payment processor)
  • Volunteer information: availability, skills, and areas of interest
  • Communication preferences: newsletter subscriptions and contact preferences
  • Form submissions: information you provide through our online forms (contact, volunteer, newsletter, donation, speaker requests, and Get Safe Fund applications)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process donations and provide tax receipts
  • Communicate with you about our programs and events
  • Coordinate volunteer activities
  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Manage our constituent database through our CRM system (Bloomerang)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and manage our organization:

  • Bloomerang: Our donor and constituent relationship management (CRM) platform. Bloomerang stores and processes constituent data securely. Their data is hosted on AWS and is SOC 2 Type 2 certified.
  • Netlify: Our website hosting provider.
  • Google reCAPTCHA: Used on our forms to prevent spam submissions.

We connect to Bloomerang via their authorized OAuth 2.0 API to manage constituent records and group assignments. This integration is used solely to organize and serve our community effectively.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), secure API authentication, and access controls.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from our communications at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through our contact form.
